Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zeocra?

Zeocra is a newly founded technology startup specializing in innovative hardware and software solutions. We are passionate about creating products that redefine possibilities and shape the future.

When was Zeocra founded?

Zeocra was founded in 2025. We are a new company with a big vision and are excited about the journey ahead.

We are currently in the research and development phase, exploring several exciting ideas in both hardware and software. Our focus is on creating elegant and powerful solutions that solve real-world problems. Stay tuned for updates on our blog!

Are you hiring?

We are always looking for talented and passionate individuals to join our team. Please visit our careers page to learn more about our culture and how to get in touch with us.

How can I get in touch?

You can get in touch with us through our contact page. We would love to hear from you, whether you have a question, a proposal, or just want to say hello.
